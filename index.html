Our Story

Once upon a time…

How did we get here? Tables de France began on the school benches in 1ère 2, when Antoine, hard-working and a good student, and Philippe, who prefers not to tire himself out too much, had a revelation: you don't need to pass parcourSup, you just need to set up your own business. So, logically, we did it! (or so we thought). For weeks we searched and searched and searched... but finally the answer came by chance when we asked some tourists what they thought of French gastronomy, and they replied “Hum...bah...heu...”. Disappointment... EUREKA! We're going on a crusade to reclaim the Throne of French cuisine! But how do we convince our potential adepts? How do we combine simplicity, originality and intensity? How do we convey our love of France? So we came up with Tables de France, a brilliant, innovative concept that makes the world's best cuisine accessible to everyone!
